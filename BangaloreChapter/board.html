Our Collective Mission

The 2025-2026 Governing Council is committed to advancing quality circle concepts, promoting total quality management practices, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement across Karnataka's diverse industrial sectors. Together, we work towards enhancing organizational performance, productivity, and customer satisfaction through collaborative quality initiatives.

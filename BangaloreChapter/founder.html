Meet Our Founders Our History

Pioneers of Quality Excellence

The Founder Directors of QCFI Bengaluru Chapter were visionary leaders who recognized the transformative power of Quality Circles and Total Quality Management in revolutionizing Karnataka's industrial landscape. Their foresight and dedication laid the foundation for what would become Karnataka's premier platform for quality excellence.

These distinguished professionals came from diverse backgrounds - academia, industry, and corporate leadership - united by a common vision: to create a sustainable ecosystem of continuous improvement and quality consciousness across Karnataka's manufacturing, IT, and service sectors.

Through their collective wisdom and relentless efforts, they established QCFI Bengaluru Chapter in 1982, creating a platform that would nurture quality professionals, promote best practices, and drive organizational excellence across Karnataka for decades to come.

The founders' legacy continues to inspire new generations of quality professionals, ensuring that their vision of quality excellence remains at the forefront of Karnataka's industrial development and innovation ecosystem.

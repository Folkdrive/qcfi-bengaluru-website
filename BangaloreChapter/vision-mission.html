Vision & Mission

Driving Quality Excellence and Developing Total Quality People Across Karnataka's Industries

Our Foundation

Quality Circle Forum of India came into existence in April 1982 as a non-profit, non-political national professional body with the purpose of creating an environment for active involvement and participation of employees in every area of human endeavor.

QCFI has expanded its scope to develop total quality people and improve quality of life through the concepts and philosophy of quality, with Quality Circles as an essential and integral part. Quality encompasses all existing and emerging concepts relevant to organizational excellence.

QCFI maintains a distinctive approach of total integrated development of all strata of people, empowering organizations and individuals across Karnataka's diverse industrial landscape.

Our Guiding Principles

The foundation that drives our commitment to quality excellence

Shared Vision

Quality Circle Forum of India (QCFI) Bengaluru Chapter is a leading national hub dedicated to spreading quality concepts, innovative problem-solving methods, and the philosophy of continuous improvement. With a strong focus on Quality Circles, we strive to enhance material, human, and spiritual development across industries, institutions, and communities. As the premier quality movement platform in Bangalore, we empower organizations to achieve excellence through employee involvement, teamwork, and sustainable growth. Join us in building a culture of quality and productivity in Karnataka and beyond.

Our Mission

We are committed to transforming organizations and individuals through quality excellence:

  • Impart training, knowledge and practice of Quality concepts with special attention to Quality Circles
  • Enable people to face challenges and achieve success in today's competitive world
  • Disseminate, share and keep abreast in the knowledge of Quality concepts
  • Create Total Quality People through comprehensive development programs

Our Strategic Objectives

Clear goals that guide our journey toward quality excellence

National Leadership in Quality

Serve as the premier national organization for promotion, propagation and advancement of Quality Circles theory and practice across Karnataka and India.

Comprehensive Training Programs

Organize specialized training and development programmes for Leaders, Facilitators and Employees at all organizational levels.

Work Life Enhancement

Improve, enrich and uplift the quality of work life while providing opportunities for continuous self-development and professional growth.

Ethical Standards

Cultivate and promote high standards of moral ethics among all members and partner organizations across Karnataka.

Knowledge Hub

Act as central clearing house for Quality Circle and related Quality concepts information, research, and best practices.

Collaborative Platform

Serve as common forum for professionals engaged in Quality Circles, facilitating knowledge sharing through seminars, workshops, and publications.

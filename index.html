About QCFI Bengaluru Chapter

The Quality Circle Forum of India, Bengaluru Chapter has been the pioneering force in promoting Quality Circle concepts and Total Quality Management practices across Karnataka's diverse industrial landscape since our establishment in 1982.

We serve as Karnataka's premier platform for quality professionals to collaborate, share best practices, and drive continuous improvement initiatives that enhance organizational performance, productivity, and customer satisfaction across multiple sectors.

Our chapter brings together professionals from IT, manufacturing, healthcare, education, and service industries to create Karnataka's most vibrant ecosystem of quality excellence and innovation.

About QCFI Bengaluru Chapter

The Quality Circle Forum of India, Bengaluru Chapter has been at the forefront of promoting quality circle concepts and facilitating their implementation across diverse industries in Karnataka since 1982.

We serve as a platform for quality professionals to collaborate, share best practices, and drive continuous improvement initiatives that enhance organizational performance and customer satisfaction.

Our chapter brings together professionals from manufacturing, IT, healthcare, and service industries to create a vibrant ecosystem of quality excellence.

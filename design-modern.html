About QCFI Bengaluru

Promoting Quality Circle activities and continuous improvement culture in organizations across Bengaluru region.

The Quality Circle Forum of India (QCFI) is a premier non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Quality Circle activities and other people-building movements across India.

The Bengaluru Chapter has been actively fostering a culture of continuous improvement and employee participation in organizations throughout the region since its inception.
